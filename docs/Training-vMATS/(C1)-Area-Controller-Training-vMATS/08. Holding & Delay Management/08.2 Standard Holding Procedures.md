# 08.2 Standard Holding Procedures

## Training Objective

To apply standard ICAO holding procedures.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Holding pattern structure
- Inbound and outbound legs
- Timing and speed considerations

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Issue holding clearances correctly
- Ensure aircraft comply with published procedures

---

## 1. Overview

A standard holding pattern consists of racetrack-shaped tracks defined by inbound course and turns.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Holding Pattern

- Inbound leg
- Outbound leg
- Standard rate turns

### 2.2 Direction of Turns

- Standard: right turns
- Non-standard: left turns as published

### 2.3 Timing

- Typically 1 minute inbound below FL140
- 1.5 minutes above FL140

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Holding Clearance

- Fix
- Level
- Inbound track
- Turn direction
- Timing if required

---

## 4. Standards

- ICAO holding procedures shall be applied

---

## 5. Phraseology

### Examples:

- "Hold at [fix], maintain FLXXX"
