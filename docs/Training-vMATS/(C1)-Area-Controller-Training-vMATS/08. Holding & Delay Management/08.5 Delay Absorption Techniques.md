# 08.5 Delay Absorption Techniques

## Training Objective

To apply methods to absorb delay without excessive use of holding.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Speed control
- Vectoring for delay
- Level restrictions

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Use appropriate techniques to absorb delay
- Minimise use of holding where possible

---

## 1. Overview

Delay can be absorbed using multiple techniques, not just holding.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Speed Control

- Reduce speed to increase spacing

### 2.2 Vectoring

- Extend track distance

### 2.3 Level Restrictions

- Delay climb or descent

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Controllers shall select the least disruptive method

---

## 4. Standards

- Safety shall not be compromised

---

## 5. Notes

- Holding is a last resort in many situations
