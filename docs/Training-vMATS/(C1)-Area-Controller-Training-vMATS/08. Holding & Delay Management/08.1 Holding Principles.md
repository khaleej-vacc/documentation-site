# 08.1 Holding Principles

## Training Objective

To understand the purpose and application of holding in an en-route environment.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Purpose of holding
- When to apply holding
- Impact on traffic flow

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Use holding to regulate traffic flow
- Apply holding when demand exceeds capacity
- Maintain separation within the hold

---

## 1. Overview

Holding is used to delay aircraft in a controlled manner when traffic demand exceeds available capacity.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Purpose of Holding

- Delay absorption
- Traffic sequencing
- Flow regulation

### 2.2 When to Use Holding

- Sector overload
- Arrival congestion
- Downstream restrictions

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Holding shall be applied before congestion becomes unmanageable
- Aircraft shall be issued clear holding instructions

---

## 4. Standards

- Separation shall be maintained at all times within the hold

---

## 5. Notes

- Late use of holding leads to instability
