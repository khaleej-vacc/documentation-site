# 08.4 Stack Management

## Training Objective

To manage multiple aircraft within a holding stack.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Vertical stacking
- Separation within holds
- Sequencing aircraft

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Assign levels to maintain separation
- Sequence aircraft for exit
- Monitor stack stability

---

## 1. Overview

Holding stacks are used to manage multiple aircraft at a single fix.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Vertical Separation

- Aircraft separated by level
- Standard vertical minima apply

### 2.2 Sequencing

- Aircraft exit hold in planned order
- Lower aircraft typically released first

### 2.3 Stability

- Stable stack reduces workload

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Stack Management

- Controllers shall maintain organised levels
- Adjust levels as required

---

## 4. Standards

- Separation shall be maintained within the stack

---

## 5. Notes

- Poor stack management leads to congestion
