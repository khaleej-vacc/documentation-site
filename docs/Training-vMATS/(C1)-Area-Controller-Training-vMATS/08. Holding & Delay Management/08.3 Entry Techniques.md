# 08.3 Entry Techniques

## Training Objective

To understand standard holding entry techniques.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Direct entry
- Teardrop entry
- Parallel entry

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Understand entry techniques used by pilots
- Anticipate aircraft positioning during entry

---

## 1. Overview

Aircraft may enter a holding pattern using different techniques depending on their inbound track.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Direct Entry

- Simplest entry
- Aircraft joins holding pattern directly

### 2.2 Teardrop Entry

- Offset entry to intercept inbound track

### 2.3 Parallel Entry

- Aircraft flies outbound before turning back

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Controller Consideration

- Controllers shall anticipate track deviations during entry

---

## 4. Standards

- Entry technique is pilot responsibility

---

## 5. Notes

- Entry phase increases complexity
