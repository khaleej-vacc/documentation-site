# 05.3 Geometry & Intercepts

## Training Objective

To understand the geometry of vectoring and how to achieve accurate intercepts.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Intercept angles
- Closure rates
- Relative positioning

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Apply appropriate intercept angles
- Avoid overshoot
- Position aircraft efficiently

---

## 1. Overview

Vectoring requires an understanding of relative geometry between aircraft and desired tracks.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Intercept Angles

- Small angles (20–30°) for gentle intercepts
- Larger angles for rapid positioning

### 2.2 Closure Rate

- Depends on speed and angle
- High closure requires early action

### 2.3 Overshoot Prevention

- Reduce intercept angle as aircraft approaches track

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Intercept Technique

- Assign initial intercept heading
- Adjust as required to capture track

---

## 4. Standards

- Intercepts shall be smooth and controlled

---

## 5. Notes

- Poor geometry leads to repeated corrections
