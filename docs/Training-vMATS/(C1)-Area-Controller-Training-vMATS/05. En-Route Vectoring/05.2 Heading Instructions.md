# 05.2 Heading Instructions

## Training Objective

To ensure correct and standardised use of heading instructions.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Heading phraseology
- Magnetic headings
- Instruction clarity

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Issue clear and concise heading instructions
- Ensure instructions are unambiguous
- Monitor compliance

---

## 1. Overview

Heading instructions are used to direct aircraft along a specified track.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Magnetic Headings

- Expressed in degrees
- Three-digit format (e.g. 090, 270)

### 2.2 Types of Instructions

- “Turn left heading XXX”
- “Turn right heading XXX”
- “Fly heading XXX”

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Issuing Headings

- Instructions shall include direction and heading where applicable
- Avoid ambiguous instructions

---

## 4. Standards

- Phraseology shall comply with ICAO standards

---

## 5. Phraseology

### Examples:

- "Turn left heading 270"
- "Fly heading 180"
- "Turn right heading 090"

---

## 6. Notes

- Late or unclear instructions increase pilot workload
