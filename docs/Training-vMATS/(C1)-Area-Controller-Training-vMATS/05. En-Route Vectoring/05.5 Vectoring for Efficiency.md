# 05.5 Vectoring for Efficiency

## Training Objective

To use vectoring to improve traffic efficiency without compromising safety.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Direct routings vs vectoring
- Track shortening
- Flow improvement

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Apply vectoring to reduce track miles where appropriate
- Balance efficiency with workload and complexity

---

## 1. Overview

Vectoring may be used to improve efficiency, but must be applied carefully.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Track Reduction

- Shorter routes reduce flight time
- May increase complexity if overused

### 2.2 Flow Improvement

- Adjust positioning to improve sequencing

### 2.3 Risk Considerations

- Efficiency shall not compromise separation

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Assess impact before issuing vectors
- Avoid unnecessary complexity

---

## 4. Standards

- Safety takes priority over efficiency

---

## 5. Notes

- Over-optimisation increases workload
