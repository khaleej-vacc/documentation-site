# 09.6 Level & Speed Agreements

## Training Objective

To apply standard coordination agreements for levels and speeds.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Standard level agreements
- Speed restrictions
- Flow integration

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Comply with agreed levels and speeds
- Coordinate deviations when required

---

## 1. Overview

Level and speed agreements standardise coordination and reduce workload.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Level Agreements

- Defined in LOAs or local procedures

### 2.2 Speed Control

- Used to manage flow and spacing

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Controllers shall adhere to agreed parameters
- Non-standard conditions shall be coordinated

---

## 4. Standards

- Agreements shall be followed unless otherwise coordinated

---

## 5. Notes

- Deviations without coordination create downstream conflicts
