# 09.3 ACC ↔ APP Coordination

## Training Objective

To ensure effective coordination between en-route and approach control units.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Arrival and departure coordination
- Level restrictions
- Transfer conditions

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Coordinate aircraft in accordance with agreed procedures
- Deliver aircraft in a stable and expected condition
- Comply with approach unit requirements

---

## 1. Overview

ACC and APP coordination ensures smooth transition between en-route and terminal environments.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Arrivals

- Aircraft shall be delivered:
  - At agreed level
  - On agreed routing
  - At appropriate speed

---

### 2.2 Departures

- ACC shall accept aircraft:
  - After departure release
  - In accordance with coordination agreements

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Coordination Requirements

- Level, speed, and route shall be coordinated where required
- Non-standard conditions shall be agreed prior to transfer

---

## 4. Standards

- Aircraft shall meet agreed transfer conditions

---

## 5. Notes

- Unstable arrivals increase workload for approach controllers
