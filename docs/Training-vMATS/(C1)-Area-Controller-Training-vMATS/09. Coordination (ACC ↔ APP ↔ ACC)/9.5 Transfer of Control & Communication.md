# 09.5 Transfer of Control & Communication

## Training Objective

To distinguish between transfer of control and transfer of communication.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Definitions of control and communication transfer
- Timing differences

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Ensure clarity of responsibility at all times
- Transfer communication appropriately

---

## 1. Overview

Transfer of control and communication may occur at different points.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Transfer of Control

- Responsibility for separation transfers

### 2.2 Transfer of Communication

- Aircraft changes frequency

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Controllers shall clearly understand when responsibility transfers
- Communication transfer shall not create ambiguity

---

## 4. Standards

- Responsibility shall always be clearly defined

---

## 5. Notes

- Confusion between control and communication is a common error
