# 09.1 General

## Training Objective

To introduce the principles of coordination between controllers and units.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Coordination requirements
- Responsibility transfer
- Communication principles

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Coordinate all relevant information prior to transfer
- Ensure clarity of responsibility at all times
- Avoid assumptions between units

---

## 1. Overview

Coordination ensures the safe and orderly transfer of aircraft between controllers and units.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Purpose of Coordination

- Maintain separation
- Ensure continuity of control
- Support efficient traffic flow

---

### 2.2 Responsibility

- The transferring controller remains responsible until transfer is complete
- The receiving controller assumes responsibility upon acceptance

---

## 3. Standards

- Coordination shall be clear, complete, and timely

---

## 4. Notes

- Lack of coordination is a major cause of operational error
