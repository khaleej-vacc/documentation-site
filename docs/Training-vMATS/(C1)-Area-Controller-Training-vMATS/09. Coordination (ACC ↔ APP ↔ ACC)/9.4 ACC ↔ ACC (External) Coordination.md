# 09.4 ACC ↔ ACC (External) Coordination

## Training Objective

To coordinate aircraft between different FIRs.

---

## Key Knowledge

- FIR boundaries
- Letters of Agreement (LOA)
- Coordination points

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Coordinate aircraft in accordance with LOAs
- Ensure compliance with boundary conditions
- Maintain separation until transfer

---

## 1. Overview

External coordination ensures safe transfer between FIRs.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Coordination Points

- Defined boundary fixes
- Transfer occurs at agreed points

---

### 2.2 LOA Compliance

- Defines:
  - Levels
  - Routes
  - Speeds

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Coordination

- Controllers shall comply with LOA requirements
- Deviations shall be coordinated in advance

---

## 4. Standards

- Aircraft shall meet agreed boundary conditions

---

## 5. Notes

- Failure to follow LOA leads to coordination breakdown
