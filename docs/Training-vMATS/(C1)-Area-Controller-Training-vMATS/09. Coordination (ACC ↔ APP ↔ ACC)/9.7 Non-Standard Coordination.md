# 09.7 Non-Standard Coordination

## Training Objective

To manage situations that fall outside standard agreements.

---

## Key Knowledge

- When coordination is required
- Communication clarity

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Coordinate all deviations from standard conditions
- Ensure mutual understanding

---

## 1. Overview

Non-standard coordination occurs when aircraft do not meet agreed conditions.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Examples

- Incorrect level
- Off-route aircraft
- Speed deviations

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Coordination

- Controllers shall communicate clearly and explicitly
- Agreement shall be confirmed

---

## 4. Standards

- Aircraft shall not be transferred without agreement

---

## 5. Notes

- Assumed coordination is unsafe
