# 09.8 Coordination Under High Workload

## Training Objective

To maintain effective coordination under high workload conditions.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Workload management
- Prioritisation

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Maintain coordination standards under pressure
- Prioritise safety-critical communication

---

## 1. Overview

High workload increases risk of coordination errors.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Prioritisation

- Safety-critical coordination first

### 2.2 Simplification

- Use standard agreements where possible

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Controllers shall avoid unnecessary coordination complexity

---

## 4. Standards

- Coordination shall remain clear and effective

---

## 5. Notes

- Workload does not justify reduced safety standards
