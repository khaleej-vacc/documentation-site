# 11.6 Loss of Separation & Recovery

## Training Objective

To manage and recover from loss of separation.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Immediate corrective actions
- Risk management

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Take immediate action to restore separation
- Minimise further risk

---

## 1. Overview

Loss of separation requires immediate corrective action.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Immediate Action

- Issue avoiding instructions  
- Increase separation  

### 2.2 Recovery

- Stabilise traffic situation  
- Prevent further conflicts  

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Controllers shall act without delay  

---

## 4. Standards

- Safety shall be restored as quickly as possible  

---

## 5. Notes

- Hesitation increases severity of situation
