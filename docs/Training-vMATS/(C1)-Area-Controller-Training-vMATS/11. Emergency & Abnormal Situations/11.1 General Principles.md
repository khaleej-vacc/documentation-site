# 11.1 General Principles

## Training Objective

To establish the principles for handling emergency and abnormal situations.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Controller priorities
- Emergency handling principles
- Decision-making under pressure

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Prioritise safety above all other considerations
- Remain calm and act decisively
- Provide maximum assistance to aircraft in difficulty

---

## 1. Overview

Emergency situations require immediate attention and effective management to ensure safety.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Priority of Action

1. Separation  
2. Emergency handling  
3. Traffic flow  

---

### 2.2 Controller Role

- Provide support and information  
- Reduce workload for the flight crew  
- Coordinate with other units  

---

## 3. Standards

- Safety shall not be compromised under any circumstances  

---

## 4. Notes

- Calm and clear communication is critical
