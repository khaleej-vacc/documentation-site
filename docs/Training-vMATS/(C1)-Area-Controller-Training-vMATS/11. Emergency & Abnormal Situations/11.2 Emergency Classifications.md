# 11.2 Emergency Classifications

## Training Objective

To identify and understand different emergency classifications.

---

## Key Knowledge

- MAYDAY
- PAN PAN
- Urgency vs distress

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Recognise emergency declarations
- Apply appropriate priority handling

---

## 1. Overview

Emergencies are classified based on severity and urgency.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Distress (MAYDAY)

- Serious and imminent danger  
- Requires immediate assistance  

---

### 2.2 Urgency (PAN PAN)

- Situation requiring attention  
- Not immediately life-threatening  

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Response

- Acknowledge immediately  
- Provide priority handling  

---

## 4. Standards

- Emergency aircraft shall receive priority over all other traffic  

---

## 5. Notes

- Delayed response increases risk
