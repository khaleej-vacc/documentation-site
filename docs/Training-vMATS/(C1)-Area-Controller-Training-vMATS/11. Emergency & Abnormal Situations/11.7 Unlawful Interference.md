# 11.7 Unlawful Interference

## Training Objective

To recognise and manage unlawful interference situations.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Hijacking indicators
- Communication procedures

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Recognise signs of unlawful interference
- Follow established procedures

---

## 1. Overview

Unlawful interference includes hijacking or unlawful acts onboard aircraft.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Indicators

- Unusual communication  
- Squawk 7500  

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Response

- Maintain communication  
- Notify appropriate authorities  

---

## 4. Standards

- Controllers shall follow established protocols  

---

## 5. Notes

- Do not escalate situation unnecessarily
