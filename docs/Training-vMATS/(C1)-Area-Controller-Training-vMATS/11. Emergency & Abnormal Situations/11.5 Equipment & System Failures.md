# 11.5 Equipment & System Failures

## Training Objective

To manage failures of ATC systems or aircraft equipment.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Radar failure
- Communication failure
- Transponder failure

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Apply alternative procedures when systems fail
- Increase separation where required

---

## 1. Overview

System failures may reduce the ability to provide radar service.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Radar Failure

- Transition to procedural control if required  

### 2.2 Communication Failure

- Apply standard lost comm procedures  

### 2.3 Transponder Failure

- Identify aircraft using alternative methods  

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Controllers shall adapt to degraded operations  

---

## 4. Standards

- Separation shall be maintained using appropriate methods  

---

## 5. Notes

- System degradation increases workload significantly
