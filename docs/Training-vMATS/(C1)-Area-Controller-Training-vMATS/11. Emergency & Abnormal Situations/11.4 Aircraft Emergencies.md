# 11.4 Aircraft Emergencies

## Training Objective

To manage aircraft experiencing emergencies.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Common emergency types
- Controller support actions

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Provide assistance as requested
- Offer suitable routing and levels
- Coordinate with relevant units

---

## 1. Overview

Aircraft emergencies may include technical failures, medical situations, or fuel issues.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Common Emergencies

- Engine failure  
- Medical emergency  
- Fuel emergency  

---

### 2.2 Controller Actions

- Provide priority handling  
- Offer nearest suitable aerodrome  
- Coordinate emergency services  

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Assistance

- Controllers shall support without overloading the crew  

---

## 4. Standards

- Assistance shall be appropriate and timely  

---

## 5. Notes

- Do not overwhelm pilots with unnecessary instructions
