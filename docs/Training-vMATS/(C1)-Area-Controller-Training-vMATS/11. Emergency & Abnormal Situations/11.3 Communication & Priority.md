# 11.3 Communication & Priority

## Training Objective

To manage communication effectively during emergencies.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Priority handling
- Frequency management
- Clarity under pressure

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Give priority to emergency aircraft
- Maintain clear and concise communication
- Reduce unnecessary frequency congestion

---

## 1. Overview

Effective communication is essential during emergency situations.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Priority

- Emergency traffic has absolute priority  

### 2.2 Frequency Control

- Limit non-essential transmissions  
- Maintain clear frequency  

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Communication

- Controllers shall use clear and direct phraseology  
- Avoid unnecessary information  

---

## 4. Standards

- Communication shall remain clear and controlled  

---

## 5. Notes

- Frequency congestion increases risk
