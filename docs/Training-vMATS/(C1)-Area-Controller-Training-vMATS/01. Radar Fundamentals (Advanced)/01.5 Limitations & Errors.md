# 01.5 Limitations & Errors

## 1. Radar Limitations

- Line-of-sight restrictions
- Terrain shielding
- Range limitations
- Update delays

---

## 2. SSR Limitations

- Incorrect squawk selection
- Altitude readout errors
- Transponder failure

---

## 3. Human Factors

- Misidentification
- Fixation
- Loss of situational awareness

---

## 4. System Errors

- Label swaps
- Track coasting
- Data dropouts

---

## 5. Mitigation

Controllers shall:

- Cross-check data
- Maintain situational awareness
- Avoid over-reliance on automation
