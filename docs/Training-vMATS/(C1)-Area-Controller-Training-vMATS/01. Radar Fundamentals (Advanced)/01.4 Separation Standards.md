# 01.4 Separation Standards

## 1. Radar Separation Minima

### Lateral Separation
- Standard: 5 NM

### Vertical Separation
- 1000 ft (RVSM airspace)
- 2000 ft (non-RVSM where applicable)

---

## 2. Application

Controllers must:

- Ensure continuous monitoring
- Anticipate loss of separation
- Apply corrective action early

---

## 3. Reduced Separation (if applicable)

- Subject to local SOPs
- Requires surveillance system performance criteria

---

## 4. Wake Turbulence Considerations

- Apply additional spacing where required
