# 07.5 Strategic vs Tactical

## Training Objective

To distinguish between strategic and tactical conflict management.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Planning vs immediate action
- Traffic flow integration

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Use strategic planning to prevent conflicts
- Apply tactical action when required

---

## 1. Overview

Conflict management occurs at two levels: strategic and tactical.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Strategic Control

- Planning ahead (10–20 minutes)
- Reduces future conflicts

### 2.2 Tactical Control

- Immediate intervention
- Resolves active conflicts

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Strategic control preferred where possible
- Tactical control used when required

---

## 4. Standards

- Controllers shall not rely solely on tactical intervention

---

## 5. Notes

- Excessive tactical control increases workload
