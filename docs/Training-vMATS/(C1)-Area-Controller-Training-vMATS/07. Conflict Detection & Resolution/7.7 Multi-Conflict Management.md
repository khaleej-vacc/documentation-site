# 07.7 Multi-Conflict Management

## Training Objective

To manage multiple simultaneous conflicts within a sector.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Prioritisation
- Conflict interaction
- Workload management

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Prioritise conflicts based on risk
- Avoid resolving one conflict at the expense of another
- Maintain overall situational awareness

---

## 1. Overview

Multiple conflicts may occur simultaneously in high-density sectors.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Prioritisation

- Highest risk conflicts addressed first

### 2.2 Interaction

- One resolution may create another conflict

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Management

- Controllers shall consider the entire traffic picture

---

## 4. Standards

- All conflicts shall be resolved without loss of separation

---

## 5. Notes

- Tunnel vision is a major risk in multi-conflict scenarios
