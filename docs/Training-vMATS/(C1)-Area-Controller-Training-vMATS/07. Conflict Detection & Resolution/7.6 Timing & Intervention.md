# 07.6 Timing & Intervention

## Training Objective

To ensure correct timing of conflict resolution actions.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Early vs late intervention
- Closure rates
- Decision timing

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Intervene early to simplify resolution
- Avoid last-minute actions

---

## 1. Overview

Timing is critical in conflict resolution.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Early Intervention

- Requires smaller corrections
- Reduces workload

### 2.2 Late Intervention

- Requires larger deviations
- Increases risk

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Decision Timing

- Controllers shall act before conflict becomes critical

---

## 4. Standards

- Interventions shall be timely and effective

---

## 5. Notes

- Late decisions are a common cause of loss of separation
