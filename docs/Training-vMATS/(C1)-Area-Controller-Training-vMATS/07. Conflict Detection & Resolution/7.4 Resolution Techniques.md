# 07.4 Resolution Techniques

## Training Objective

To apply appropriate techniques to resolve conflicts safely and efficiently.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Lateral resolution
- Vertical resolution
- Speed control

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Select the most effective resolution method
- Apply minimum required intervention
- Avoid creating secondary conflicts

---

## 1. Overview

Conflict resolution involves altering aircraft trajectory to maintain separation.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Lateral Resolution

- Heading changes
- Effective for most conflict types

### 2.2 Vertical Resolution

- Level changes
- Useful for opposite direction and crossing conflicts

### 2.3 Speed Control

- Fine adjustments
- Supports sequencing and spacing

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Selection of Technique

- Based on conflict type and geometry
- Consider downstream impact

---

## 4. Standards

- Resolution shall ensure separation is maintained

---

## 5. Notes

- Overcomplicated solutions increase workload
