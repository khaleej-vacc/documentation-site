# 07.8 Common Errors

## Training Objective

To identify common errors in conflict detection and resolution.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Typical controller mistakes
- Risk factors

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Recognise and avoid common errors
- Maintain disciplined control techniques

---

## 1. Overview

Errors in conflict management can lead to loss of separation.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Late Detection

- Failure to identify conflicts early

### 2.2 Insufficient Action

- Heading changes too small

### 2.3 Fixation

- Focusing on one aircraft

### 2.4 Over-Control

- Unnecessary complexity

---

## 3. Standards

- Controllers shall apply disciplined and effective control

---

## 4. Notes

- Most errors are linked to poor situational awareness
