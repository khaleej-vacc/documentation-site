# 03.5 Decision Making

## Training Objective

To ensure effective and timely decision-making in a dynamic environment.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Tactical vs strategic decisions
- Risk assessment
- Intervention timing

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Make timely decisions to resolve conflicts
- Apply the safest and most efficient solution
- Avoid unnecessary complexity

---

## 1. Overview

Decision-making in ATC involves selecting safe and effective actions under time pressure.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Tactical Decisions

- Immediate actions
- Conflict resolution

### 2.2 Strategic Decisions

- Planning ahead
- Traffic flow management

### 2.3 Risk Assessment

- Evaluate consequences
- Choose safest option

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Intervention Timing

- Early intervention preferred
- Avoid last-minute actions

---

## 4. Standards

- Decisions shall prioritise safety over efficiency
- Solutions shall be clear and manageable

---

## 5. Notes

- Late decisions increase workload and risk
