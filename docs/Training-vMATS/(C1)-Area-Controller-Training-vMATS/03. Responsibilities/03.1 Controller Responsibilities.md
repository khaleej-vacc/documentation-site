# 03.1 Controller Responsibilities

## Training Objective

To define the core responsibilities of an Area Controller in a radar environment.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Legal and operational responsibility of ATC
- Scope of control within a sector
- Obligation to maintain separation
- Duty of care

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Provide a safe, orderly and expeditious flow of traffic
- Maintain separation at all times
- Issue clear and timely instructions
- Monitor aircraft continuously

---

## 1. Overview

The Area Controller is responsible for the safe and efficient management of all aircraft within their assigned sector.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Safety Responsibility

- Safety is the primary responsibility at all times
- No operational decision shall compromise separation

### 2.2 Control Responsibility

- Applies to all identified aircraft within the sector
- Includes monitoring, instruction, and intervention

### 2.3 Duty of Care

- Controllers shall act to prevent unsafe situations
- Early intervention is required where risk is identified

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Provision of Control

- Aircraft shall not be left unmanaged
- Instructions shall be issued proactively

---

## 4. Standards

- Separation minima shall be maintained at all times
- Controllers are accountable for instructions issued

---

## 5. Notes

- Passive control leads to conflict development
