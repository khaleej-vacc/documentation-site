# 03.4 Situational Awareness

## Training Objective

To develop and maintain an accurate mental picture of traffic.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Traffic picture development
- Projection and anticipation
- Risk recognition

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Maintain continuous awareness of all aircraft
- Anticipate conflicts before they occur
- Monitor trends, not just positions

---

## 1. Overview

Situational awareness is the controller’s ability to understand current and future traffic situations.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Traffic Picture

- Position, level, speed, intent

### 2.2 Projection

- Predict future positions
- Identify conflicts early

### 2.3 Monitoring

- Continuous scan of sector
- Avoid fixation

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Scan Technique

- Systematic radar scan
- Prioritise high-risk aircraft

---

## 4. Standards

- Conflicts should be identified early
- Controllers shall not rely solely on system alerts

---

## 5. Notes

- Loss of awareness is a primary cause of incidents
