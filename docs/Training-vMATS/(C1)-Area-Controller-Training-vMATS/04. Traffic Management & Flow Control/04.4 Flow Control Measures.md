# 04.4 Flow Control Measures

## Training Objective

To apply techniques that regulate traffic flow and reduce complexity.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Speed control
- Vectoring
- Level restrictions
- Direct routings

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Use appropriate techniques to regulate traffic flow
- Apply minimal intervention required to maintain control

---

## 1. Overview

Flow control measures are used to maintain safe and efficient traffic movement.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Speed Control

- Primary tool for minor adjustments

### 2.2 Vectoring

- Used to resolve conflicts or manage spacing

### 2.3 Level Restrictions

- Prevent conflicts and manage flow

### 2.4 Direct Routing

- Improves efficiency but may increase complexity

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application of Measures

- Controllers shall select the simplest effective solution
- Actions shall not create secondary conflicts

---

## 4. Standards

- Safety shall not be compromised for efficiency

---

## 5. Notes

- Overuse of vectoring increases workload
