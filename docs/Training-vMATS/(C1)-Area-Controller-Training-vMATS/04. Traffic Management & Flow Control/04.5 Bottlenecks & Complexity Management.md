# 04.5 Bottlenecks & Complexity Management

## Training Objective

To identify and manage traffic bottlenecks and complexity within a sector.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Bottleneck locations
- Conflict hotspots
- Complexity drivers

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Identify high-risk areas within the sector
- Apply proactive measures to reduce complexity
- Manage traffic before bottlenecks form

---

## 1. Overview

Bottlenecks occur where traffic converges, increasing the risk of conflict and workload.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Bottlenecks

- Entry/exit points
- Crossing routes
- Level-off points

### 2.2 Complexity Drivers

- High traffic density
- Mixed climb/descent profiles
- Weather deviations

### 2.3 Conflict Hotspots

- Predictable areas of risk
- Require increased attention

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Complexity Management

- Early intervention shall be applied
- Traffic shall be organised before reaching bottlenecks

---

## 4. Standards

- Controllers shall maintain manageable workload at all times

---

## 5. Notes

- Late action leads to sector overload
