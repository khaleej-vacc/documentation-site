# 12.8 Human Factors

## Training Objective

To understand human performance limitations in ATC.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Fatigue
- Stress
- Attention limits

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Recognise signs of overload
- Maintain effective scanning techniques
- Manage stress and workload

---

## 1. Overview

Human factors influence controller performance and safety.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Fatigue

- Reduces awareness and reaction time

### 2.2 Stress

- Impacts decision-making

### 2.3 Attention

- Limited capacity for monitoring

---

## 3. Standards

- Controllers shall operate within safe limits of performance

---

## 4. Notes

- Most incidents involve human factors
