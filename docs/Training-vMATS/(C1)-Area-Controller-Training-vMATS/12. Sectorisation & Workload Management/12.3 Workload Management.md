# 12.3 Workload Management

## Training Objective

To manage workload effectively within an en-route sector.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Workload vs traffic count
- Tactical vs strategic workload
- Cognitive limits

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Maintain workload within safe limits
- Prioritise tasks effectively
- Anticipate workload increases

---

## 1. Overview

Workload is determined by complexity, not just the number of aircraft.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Tactical Workload

- Immediate control actions
- Conflict resolution

### 2.2 Strategic Workload

- Planning ahead
- Flow management

---

### 2.3 Workload Drivers

- Crossing traffic
- Climb/descent profiles
- Speed differences
- Weather

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Workload Management

- Controllers shall simplify traffic where possible
- Early action shall reduce workload

---

## 4. Standards

- Controllers shall maintain situational awareness at all times

---

## 5. Notes

- High workload reduces decision-making quality
