# 12.7 Prioritisation Techniques

## Training Objective

To prioritise tasks effectively under workload.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Task hierarchy
- Decision-making under pressure

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall prioritise:

1. Separation  
2. Conflict resolution  
3. Coordination  
4. Efficiency  

---

## 1. Overview

Effective prioritisation ensures safety in high workload situations.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Task Management

- Focus on safety-critical tasks first

---

## 3. Standards

- Lower priority tasks shall not compromise safety

---

## 4. Notes

- Poor prioritisation leads to loss of control
