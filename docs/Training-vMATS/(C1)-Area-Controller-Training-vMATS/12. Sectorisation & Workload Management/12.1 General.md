# 12.1 General

## Training Objective

To introduce the principles of sectorisation and workload management within an ACC environment.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Sector structure
- Workload factors
- Capacity limitations

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Operate within sector limits
- Maintain manageable workload
- Apply effective control techniques

---

## 1. Overview

Sectorisation divides airspace into manageable areas to ensure safe and efficient control.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Purpose

- Distribute workload
- Maintain safety
- Improve efficiency

---

## 3. Standards

- Controllers shall not exceed manageable workload

---

## 4. Notes

- Poor workload management leads to loss of situational awareness
