# 12.5 Capacity & Demand

## Training Objective

To understand the relationship between traffic demand and sector capacity.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Sector capacity limits
- Demand fluctuations
- Flow imbalance

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Monitor demand continuously
- Take action before capacity is exceeded

---

## 1. Overview

Capacity is the maximum workload a sector can safely manage.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Capacity

- Defined by complexity, not numbers

### 2.2 Demand

- Traffic entering the sector

---

### 2.3 Imbalance

- Occurs when demand exceeds capacity

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Management

- Apply delay or rerouting where required

---

## 4. Standards

- Sector capacity shall not be exceeded

---

## 5. Notes

- Late action leads to sector overload
