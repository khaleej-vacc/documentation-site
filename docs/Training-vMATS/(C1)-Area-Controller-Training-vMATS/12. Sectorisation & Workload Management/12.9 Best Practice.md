# 12.9 Best Practice

## Training Objective

To reinforce effective sector and workload management techniques.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Efficient control techniques
- Proactive workload management
- Situational awareness

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Maintain a stable and predictable traffic environment
- Act early to prevent overload
- Apply simple and effective solutions

---

## 1. Overview

Best practice ensures safe, efficient, and professional control within an ACC environment.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Stability

- Maintain consistent traffic flow  
- Avoid unnecessary changes  

---

### 2.2 Simplicity

- Use the simplest effective solution  
- Avoid over-complicating control  

---

### 2.3 Proactive Control

- Anticipate future situations  
- Act before problems develop  

---

### 2.4 Awareness

- Maintain full situational awareness at all times  
- Monitor the entire sector, not individual aircraft  

---

### 2.5 Coordination

- Communicate early and clearly  
- Avoid surprises for adjacent sectors  

---

## 3. Standards

- Controllers shall operate proactively and efficiently  
- Safety shall remain the highest priority  

---

## 4. Notes

- Good control is proactive, not reactive  
- Effective controllers manage workload before it becomes critical
