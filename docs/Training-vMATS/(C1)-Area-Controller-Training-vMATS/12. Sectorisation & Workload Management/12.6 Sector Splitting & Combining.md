# 12.6 Sector Splitting & Combining

## Training Objective

To manage sector configuration based on workload.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Sector splitting
- Bandboxing
- Dynamic configuration

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Recognise when workload requires sector split
- Operate effectively in combined sectors

---

## 1. Overview

Sector configuration may change based on traffic demand.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Sector Splitting

- Reduces workload per controller

### 2.2 Bandboxing

- Combines sectors under one controller

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Changes shall be coordinated and controlled

---

## 4. Standards

- Sector configuration shall support safe operations

---

## 5. Notes

- Late splitting increases risk significantly
