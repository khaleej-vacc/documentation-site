# 06.4 Crossing Traffic & Level Changes

## Training Objective

To safely manage aircraft whose vertical profiles intersect.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Crossing conflict geometry
- Timing of level changes
- Vertical vs lateral solutions

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Identify crossing conflicts early
- Apply vertical or lateral solutions as appropriate
- Monitor until conflict is resolved

---

## 1. Overview

Crossing traffic presents a high risk of conflict when aircraft climb or descend through occupied levels.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Conflict Geometry

- Occurs when aircraft paths intersect vertically and laterally

### 2.2 Timing

- Early action reduces required intervention

### 2.3 Solution Options

- Stop climb/descent
- Assign alternative level
- Apply vectoring

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Conflict Resolution

- Issue level restriction or alternative clearance
- Monitor until separation assured

---

## 4. Standards

- Separation shall not be compromised during level change

---

## 5. Notes

- Crossing conflicts are a common source of error
