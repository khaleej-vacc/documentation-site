# 06.3 Descent Management

## Training Objective

To manage aircraft descents in a safe and controlled manner.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Descent profiles
- Timing of descent
- Interaction with lower traffic

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Issue descent instructions at appropriate time
- Ensure no conflict with aircraft below
- Sequence descending traffic effectively

---

## 1. Overview

Descent management involves controlling when and how aircraft descend to lower levels.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Descent Profiles

- Descent rates are generally higher than climb rates
- Aircraft may require early descent planning

### 2.2 Traffic Below

- Aircraft below must be protected at all times

### 2.3 Continuous Descent

- Preferred for efficiency where possible

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Descent Clearance

- "Descend FLXXX"
- May include restrictions

---

## 4. Standards

- Separation shall be maintained throughout descent

---

## 5. Notes

- Late descent creates compression and workload
