# 06.2 Climb Management

## Training Objective

To manage aircraft climbs safely and efficiently within a controlled environment.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Climb rates
- Level allocation
- Traffic integration

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Issue climb clearances in accordance with traffic conditions
- Ensure no conflict with aircraft above
- Monitor climb progress

---

## 1. Overview

Climb management ensures aircraft reach cruising levels without compromising separation.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Climb Profiles

- Aircraft climb rates vary by type and weight
- Rate reduces at higher levels

### 2.2 Traffic Above

- Aircraft above must be considered before issuing climb

### 2.3 Step Climbs

- Used when immediate climb is not possible

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Climb Clearance

- "Climb FLXXX"
- May include restrictions if required

---

## 4. Standards

- Separation shall be maintained throughout climb

---

## 5. Notes

- Unmonitored climbs can lead to vertical conflict
