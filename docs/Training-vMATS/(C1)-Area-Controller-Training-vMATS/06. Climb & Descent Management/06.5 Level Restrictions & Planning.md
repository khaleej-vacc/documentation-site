# 06.5 Level Restrictions & Planning

## Training Objective

To apply level restrictions and plan vertical profiles effectively.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Level capping
- Traffic planning
- Integration with flow management

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Apply level restrictions to manage traffic
- Plan climbs and descents in advance
- Prevent future conflicts through early planning

---

## 1. Overview

Level planning is used to organise traffic vertically and reduce complexity.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Level Capping

- Restrict climb or descent temporarily
- Used to maintain separation

### 2.2 Vertical Planning

- Plan ahead based on traffic flow
- Avoid reactive control

### 2.3 Flow Integration

- Align vertical profiles with sector traffic

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Restrictions shall be clear and achievable
- Adjustments shall be made as required

---

## 4. Standards

- Controllers shall maintain a structured vertical profile

---

## 5. Notes

- Poor planning leads to last-minute conflict resolution
