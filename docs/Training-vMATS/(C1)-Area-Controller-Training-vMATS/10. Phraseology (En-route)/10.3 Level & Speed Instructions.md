# 10.3 Level & Speed Instructions

## Training Objective

To issue clear and correct level and speed instructions.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Level assignment
- Speed control phraseology

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Issue level instructions clearly
- Apply speed control effectively

---

## 1. Overview

Level and speed instructions are critical for maintaining separation and managing flow.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Level Instructions

- "Climb FLXXX"
- "Descend FLXXX"
- "Maintain FLXXX"

### 2.2 Speed Instructions

- "Reduce speed XXX knots"
- "Maintain XXX knots"
- "Minimum clean speed"

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Instructions shall be unambiguous
- Controllers shall confirm correct readback

---

## 4. Standards

- Phraseology shall comply with ICAO standards

---

## 5. Notes

- Incorrect level or speed instructions can lead to loss of separation
