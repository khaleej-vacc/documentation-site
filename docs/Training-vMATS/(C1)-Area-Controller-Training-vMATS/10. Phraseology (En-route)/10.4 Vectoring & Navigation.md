# 10.4 Vectoring & Navigation

## Training Objective

To issue correct heading and navigation instructions.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Heading phraseology
- Direct routing
- Navigation instructions

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Issue accurate heading instructions
- Provide navigation guidance as required

---

## 1. Overview

Vectoring and navigation instructions are used to control aircraft track and positioning.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Heading Instructions

- "Turn left heading XXX"
- "Turn right heading XXX"
- "Fly heading XXX"

### 2.2 Direct Routing

- "Proceed direct [waypoint]"

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Controllers shall ensure instructions are clear and achievable

---

## 4. Standards

- Phraseology shall remain standardised

---

## 5. Notes

- Ambiguous instructions increase pilot workload
