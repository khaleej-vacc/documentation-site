# 10.5 Coordination Phraseology

## Training Objective

To apply correct phraseology during coordination between controllers.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Coordination terminology
- Agreement confirmation

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Use clear and concise coordination language
- Confirm agreements explicitly

---

## 1. Overview

Coordination phraseology ensures mutual understanding between controllers.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Standard Coordination

- Level, route, speed confirmation

### 2.2 Agreement

- Explicit confirmation required

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Controllers shall avoid ambiguity during coordination

---

## 4. Standards

- Coordination shall be clear and confirmed

---

## 5. Notes

- Miscommunication between controllers increases risk
