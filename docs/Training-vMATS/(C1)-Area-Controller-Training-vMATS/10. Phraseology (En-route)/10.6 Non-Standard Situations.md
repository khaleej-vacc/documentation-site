# 10.6 Non-Standard Situations

## Training Objective

To manage communication in abnormal or non-standard situations.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Plain language use
- Emergency communication

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Use plain language when required
- Maintain clarity under abnormal conditions

---

## 1. Overview

Non-standard situations may require deviation from standard phraseology.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Plain Language

- Used when standard phraseology is insufficient

### 2.2 Emergency Situations

- Clear and direct communication required

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Controllers shall prioritise clarity over strict phraseology when required

---

## 4. Standards

- Communication shall remain clear and unambiguous

---

## 5. Notes

- Overuse of non-standard phraseology increases confusion
