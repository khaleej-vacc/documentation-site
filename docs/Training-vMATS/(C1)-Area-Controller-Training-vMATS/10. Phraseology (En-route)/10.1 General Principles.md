# 10.1 General Principles

## Training Objective

To establish the principles of standard phraseology in an en-route environment.

---

## Key Knowledge

- ICAO phraseology standards
- Clarity and brevity
- Readback requirements

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Use standard ICAO phraseology at all times
- Speak clearly and concisely
- Avoid non-standard or informal language

---

## 1. Overview

Standard phraseology ensures consistent and unambiguous communication between controllers and pilots.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Clarity

- Instructions shall be clear and easily understood

### 2.2 Brevity

- Communication shall be concise without loss of meaning

### 2.3 Standardisation

- Only approved phraseology shall be used

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Readback

- Controllers shall ensure correct readback of critical instructions:
  - Levels
  - Headings
  - Speeds
  - Clearances

---

## 4. Standards

- Non-standard phraseology shall be avoided

---

## 5. Notes

- Poor phraseology leads to misunderstanding and increased workload
