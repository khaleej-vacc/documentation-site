# 10.2 Radar Control Phraseology

## Training Objective

To apply standard phraseology for radar control services.

---

## Key Knowledge

- Identification phraseology
- Radar service provision
- Traffic information

---

## Operational Application

Controllers shall:

- Use correct phraseology when identifying aircraft
- Provide radar services clearly
- Issue traffic information where required

---

## 1. Overview

Radar phraseology is used when providing surveillance-based services.

---

## 2. Core Content

### 2.1 Identification

- "Radar contact"
- "Identified"

### 2.2 Traffic Information

- "Traffic, [clock position], [distance], [level], [direction]"

---

## 3. Procedures

### 3.1 Application

- Phraseology shall match the service being provided

---

## 4. Standards

- Controllers shall use ICAO standard terms only

---

## 5. Notes

- Incorrect phraseology can cause confusion in busy environments
